Houses of Hawthorne

Hawthorne has a wide variaty of homes avaliable to buy or rent, ranging from apartments, townhouses, single family homes, and multi family homes. Most homes currently avaliable on the market to buy range from $200K to $500K, but some homes avaliable cost more due to their larger size or location. If you're planning on moving to town on a budget, Hawthorne has many options for apartments in all parts of town. Hawthorne is a small town, so you're never too far away from anything. Whether you're buying or renting, Hawthorne has a place to live for anybody.

  • Apartments
    • Hawthorne Square Apartment Complex
    • Arlington Arms
    • Lafayette Park Apartments
  • Single Family Homes
    • Within 5 minutes of downtown, and either train station or bus stop
    • Homes with 2-4 bedrooms
    • Homes with 1-3 bathrooms
  • Townhouses
    • Diamond Brook Hollow
    • Hawthorne Commons
    • Lafayette Hills Townhome Associations
