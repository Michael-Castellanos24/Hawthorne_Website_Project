Parks

Goffle Brook Park
Enjoy the beautiful sights of the town's main park. This park stretches nearly the entire length of Hawthorne, with a walking trail you can take to see the entire park. In the south, you'll find the beautiful "8 acre woods" which features multiple dirt pathways around all parts of the woods. Here you can enjoy the sights and sounds of Hawthorne's nature and natural beauty. Further north, you'll find multiple playgrounds and sports fields, as well as the historic Rea Mansion. At the north end of the park, you'll find the Arnold Pond with a pathway taking you around the shoreline. Goffle Brook Park hosts several events during the summer time, including "Paws in the Park", and the annual "Tricky Tray".
Diamond Bridge Park
Located at the bottom part of Goffle Brook Park, is the popular Diamond Bridge Park. This section features the already mentioned 8-acre woods, as well as an area where you can barbaque food from one of the public grills around the area. Baseball diamonds, basketball courts, and soccer fields are also located here for games to be played on. The park also features a dog park, as well as a huge newly built playground.
Wagaraw Field
Wagaraw field is located south, right along the Passaic River. The park features a playground, and four baseball diamonds for games to be played on. A snack stand is also located here, and is open on busy game days. Further down the road and past the railroad tracks, is the Hawthorne Gun Club, where anyone can practice their aim at a protected and safe shooting range.

Recreation

Hawthorne Five Movie Theater
Looking for some entertainment in town during the weekends? Check out all the latest movies at the Hawthorne Five movie theater. Enjoy a movie in one of the five theaters with family or friends. Whats a movie without a snack? Hawthorne Five offers a variaty of snacks and drinks. Of course you have delicious popcorn and soft drinks to choose from, but also you can order candy, nachos or ice cream.
Puzo's Family Restuarant
Enjoy an Italian dinner at the popular Puzo's Family Restaurant. Here, you can order many kinds of delicious foods. Pizza is what they're known for, with many different flavors of pizza avaliable for anyone to eat. Puzos also has many different kinds of salads and pasta dishes too. They also have a bar with a wide selection of alcoholic beverages. Weekends tend to be when the resturant is more crowded, so it's best to arrive early or book a reservation to avoid a long wait time. Puzos also does takeout orders if you don't feel safe sitting near lots of people too.
Hawthorne Pool
If your in town during the summer time and are looking to cool off, come visit the Hawthorne Pool. Residents of Hawthorne and North Haledon can sign up to become members, or pay between $15 - $20 to join just for the day. Non-residents are allowed to stop by too. The pool is open seven days a week, between Memorial Day, and Labor Day. If you're feeling hungry or thirsty, visit the snack cart for a tasty treat! Outside the main pool area are multiple tennis courts which are free for anyone to play games on.
